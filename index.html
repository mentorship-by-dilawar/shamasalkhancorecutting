Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of core cutting and concrete cutting services to meet all your construction and renovation needs in Dubai.

Core Drilling

Precision core drilling services for creating circular openings in concrete structures for various applications like plumbing, electrical work, and ventilation.

Concrete Sawing

Professional concrete sawing services for precise cutting of walls, floors, and other concrete structures for renovations, modifications, or demolitions.

Wall Cutting

Specialized wall cutting services for creating doorways, windows, or other openings in concrete walls with precision and minimal disruption.

Floor Sawing

Efficient floor sawing services for creating expansion joints, removing sections of concrete floors, or preparing for new installations.

Concrete Demolition

Controlled concrete demolition services for safe and efficient removal of concrete structures, walls, floors, or foundations.

Concrete Scanning

Non-destructive concrete scanning services to identify embedded objects like rebar, conduits, or pipes before cutting or drilling operations.
